Book the Cobblestone Backroom

Hi there — thanks for your interest in booking the Cobblestone Backroom. Before you fill in the form below, here's everything you need to know about the Backroom. Have a read through, then pick an available date from our calendar and send us your inquiry. The Cobblestone staff will get back to you within 2–3 working days.

⭐
Venue Fee — €150

Includes use of the room, our house sound engineer if needed, and a staffed bar for the night. This fee is payable directly to Shane on the night.

🚪
Door Person — €50

We can provide a door person for €50, payable to the Cobblestone. Or bring your own — no charge. We can provide a cash float.

👥
Capacity — 75

Max 75 admissions. Normally set up as ~50 seated / 25 standing with a mix of tables, chairs, and high benches. Layout can be adjusted on request.

🕙
Stage Curfew Times

Mon–Thu: last music by 11:30pm
Fri–Sat: last music by 12:30am
Sunday: last music by 11pm
Gigs typically wrap 30 min before last orders.

Door Times

We recommend doors at 6:30–7:30pm with music starting 30–60 minutes after. This is entirely up to you, though.

Ticketing

Ticketing is the responsibility of the act. We recommend Eventbrite or Ticket Tailor for advance ticket sales. We do not provide a card machine at the door — please bring your own if charging on the night.

No Drum Backline Available

The Backroom does not have a drum kit or backline. You will need to hire or bring your own.

Backline hire: smdrental.com  ·  soundhire.ie  ·  littonlane.ie

Location & Access

77 King St North, Dublin 7, D07 TP22

Access via Red Cow Lane — enter through the Cobblestone Pub.

Parking: free after 7pm & Sundays on Red Zone. Payzone app accepted.

Booking the Upstairs space? The Upstairs room is not suitable for gigs or large events. It has a smaller capacity, and sound from the downstairs bar carries through — making it unsuitable for amplified music or performances. It works well for classes, rehearsals, small private events, and filming. Please only select Upstairs for those types of events.
